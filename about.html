About What Are Best

We started WhatAreBest because we were tired of "review" sites that are really just affiliate link farms. You know the ones — they've never touched the products they recommend, they rank whatever pays the highest commission, and the "reviews" are rewritten Amazon descriptions.

We do things differently. We research materials, construction methods, warranty claims, and real owner feedback from forums and communities — not just star ratings that can be bought. When we recommend something, it's because we'd actually buy it ourselves.

Our focus areas include automotive accessories, home essentials, outdoor gear, and tools — categories where quality actually matters and where bad purchases waste real money.

Transparency: We participate in the Amazon Associates program, which means we earn a small commission when you buy through our links. This never influences our recommendations. We'd rather lose a commission than recommend something that sucks.
