About What Are Best

An independent review network you can actually trust

What Are Best is an independent review network spanning 16 specialized websites across automotive, home, kitchen, tech, pets, and outdoor categories. We test products, compare them honestly, and tell you what's actually worth buying — no paid placements, no sponsored rankings.

How We Work

Every product we recommend has been tested by our editorial team. We buy products at retail (or accept review units that we disclose), use them in real-world conditions, and evaluate them against the competition. Our reviews include genuine pros and cons — because nothing is perfect, and pretending otherwise doesn't help you make a decision.

Our rankings are based on performance, not payment. No brand can pay for a higher position. When a product earns our #1 spot, it's because it outperformed everything else we tested.

What Are Best is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click a link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our rankings or recommendations — our editorial team independently tests and evaluates every product.

Some links on our site go directly to manufacturer websites (like bartact.com or bowtiefilters.com). These are included because we believe they offer the best purchasing experience for those specific products, not because of any affiliate arrangement.

Our goal is simple: help you find the best products. If we wouldn't recommend it to a friend, we won't recommend it to you.
