Best Rivian R1T Accessories (2026)

Updated April 2026 · Compared practical upgrades for charging, cargo, protection, and daily usability

The Rivian R1T is in that awkward category where the truck itself is excellent, the owners are willing to spend money, and the accessory market is still figuring itself out. That means you get a lot of overpriced "lifestyle" junk, a handful of genuinely useful upgrades, and too many generic products pretending to be R1T-specific when they obviously are not.

If you own an R1T, the best accessories are not the flashy ones. They are the ones that fix the truck's weak spots in everyday use: floor protection, bed security, charging flexibility, smarter storage, and keeping the interior from getting beat up by real life. Rivian owners tend to actually use their trucks for road trips, camping, hauling gear, and commuting, which means the right accessories pay for themselves pretty quickly.

These are the R1T accessories I would actually buy, starting with the most useful upgrade for most owners.

What Matters Most for R1T Accessories

Buy for the truck you actually use

The mistake a lot of R1T owners make is buying accessories for the version of themselves they imagine. The truck ends up with expensive add-ons for a lifestyle that happens twice a year, while the genuinely useful daily upgrades get ignored. Start with protection and utility first. Cosmetics can wait.

Be skeptical of "Rivian tax" pricing

Because the aftermarket is still relatively thin, some brands charge a premium just for saying the product fits a Rivian. That does not automatically make it better. In a lot of cases, you are better off buying a proven universal organizer or charging accessory than overpaying for a mediocre R1T-branded one.

Fitment matters more than hype

The best R1T accessories are the ones that fit right and stay useful for years. If it is a bed piece, floor protection item, or screen accessory, fit is the whole story. A supposedly premium accessory that rattles, slides, or looks hacked together is not premium. It is just expensive.

The Bottom Line

The best Rivian R1T accessory for most people is a set of custom-fit all-weather floor mats, because they protect an expensive truck from everyday abuse and you will appreciate them constantly. After that, prioritize bed protection, charging flexibility, and smart storage. The R1T does not need a bunch of gimmicks. It needs a few smart upgrades that make a very good truck easier to live with.
