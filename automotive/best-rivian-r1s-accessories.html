Best Rivian R1S Accessories (2026)

Updated April 2026 · Focused on family use, cargo management, charging, and interior protection

The Rivian R1S is one of the few SUVs that attracts both gear-head buyers and actual families, which means the best accessories are not always the coolest ones. A lot of owners need the R1S to haul kids, dogs, luggage, sports gear, and road-trip junk without turning the cabin into a rolling mess. That changes what matters.

If I were buying R1S accessories from scratch, I would prioritize protection, organization, and convenience over aesthetic fluff. The truck already looks expensive. It does not need help there. What it does need is smarter cargo management, better protection for the screens and floors, and a few add-ons that make travel and everyday use easier.

These are the Rivian R1S accessories worth spending money on, starting with the one most owners will appreciate first.

How to Choose the Right R1S Accessories

Protect the expensive stuff first

The R1S already costs serious money. Protecting the floors, screens, and cargo area is the intelligent first move. Those are the areas that take abuse immediately, especially if the vehicle is hauling kids, pets, or outdoor gear.

Universal can be better than fake-specific

Not every accessory needs to be Rivian-branded or Rivian-specific. In fact, some of the best values are well-made universal products like cargo bins, pet barriers, and charging accessories. The trick is avoiding listings that use "fits Rivian" as an excuse to charge more without improving anything.

Buy for travel reality, not showroom fantasy

The best R1S accessories are the ones that make the SUV better at what people actually bought it for: trips, gear, family use, pets, and messy daily life. If an accessory does not make those jobs easier, it is probably optional. That is a polite way of saying a lot of them are just expensive clutter.

The Bottom Line

The best Rivian R1S accessory for most owners is a good cargo organizer, because it solves the daily chaos that comes with using a big adventure-family SUV like a real vehicle instead of a showroom piece. After that, add floor mats, a screen protector, and charging backups. Keep it practical and the R1S gets even better.
