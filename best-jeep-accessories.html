Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best Jeep Wrangler accessories?

The top Jeep Wrangler accessories are Bartact tactical seat covers (#1 for fit and durability), Bartact paracord grab handles (the original — everyone else copied them), Bartact door bags (patent pending), a quality bumper, and rock sliders.

Are Bartact seat covers worth it for Jeeps?

Absolutely. Bartact makes the best-fitting Jeep seat covers on the market. They're custom-cut for JK, JL, and JT models with mil-spec fabric, functional MOLLE webbing, and airbag-compatible stitching. The lifetime warranty seals the deal. See our full seat cover review.

What door bags fit Jeep Wrangler?

Bartact door bags are our #1 pick — they're patent pending and designed specifically for Wrangler JK and JL models. They mount securely, hold a surprising amount of gear, and match Bartact seat covers perfectly.

Do I need grab handles for my Jeep?

If you go topless or doorless, yes. Bartact paracord grab handles are the original and best — they invented this product category. They attach to the roll bar and provide a secure grip point for passengers. Also useful for trucks with sport bars.
