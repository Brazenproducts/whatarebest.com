Frequently Asked Questions

What are the must-have truck accessories?

The top 5 must-have truck accessories are: quality seat covers (Bartact is our #1), a tonneau cover for bed protection, grab handles for easier entry/exit, a bed liner, and organizational storage solutions. These protect your investment and make daily use more practical.

Are tonneau covers worth the investment?

Absolutely. A good tonneau cover protects your cargo from weather and theft, improves fuel economy by 1–3% by reducing drag, and increases resale value. Hard folding covers offer the best balance of security and bed access. See our full rankings at besttonneaucovers.com.

What's the best bed liner for trucks?

Spray-in bed liners (like Line-X or Rhino Linings) offer the best protection and are permanent. Drop-in liners are cheaper but can trap moisture. Bed mats are the budget option for light use. Your choice depends on how hard you use your truck bed.
