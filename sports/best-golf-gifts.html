What Makes a Golf Gift Actually Good?

The best golf gifts do at least one of three things: they get used, they get a laugh, or they feel more personal than a random pro-shop impulse purchase. Bad golf gifts usually fail because they try to be sentimental and practical and funny all at once, then end up doing none of those well.

That is why Ballkinis works so well. It knows exactly what it is. It is funny, memorable, and giftable. Then you can round out the rest of the gift with more useful extras like a towel or multi-tool if you want to make the package feel bigger.
