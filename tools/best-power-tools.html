What to Look For in a Power Tool

Battery platform matters more than most people think. Once you commit to 18V/20V from one brand, you are buying into that ecosystem. Pick a platform with a large accessory range, widely available batteries, and a track record of reliability. Ryobi has the biggest homeowner ecosystem. Milwaukee and DeWalt dominate professional use.

Brushless motors are worth paying for. They run cooler, last longer, and are more efficient. Any modern tool worth buying should be brushless at this point, especially above the budget tier.

The Bottom Line

Milwaukee M18 Fuel is the best power tool system for most people in 2026 if you want professional-grade reliability. For homeowners who want solid performance without premium pricing, the Ryobi ONE+ system is the smart budget pick. Either way, buy once and stop thinking about it.
