The Realistic Home Office Priority Order

If you are building a home office from scratch, buy in this order: chair first, monitor second, desk third, keyboard and webcam last. The chair affects how you feel by hour two. The monitor affects how you feel by hour one. Everything else is incremental improvement. Spend the most money on the things closest to your body and your face.

The Bottom Line

The ErgoChair Pro is the single best home office investment in 2026 because a bad chair ruins your day before you even open a browser. After that, a good monitor and a standing desk are the upgrades that genuinely change how you feel at the end of a work day.
